Admission Details

Admission Details:

Iqra Education Pblic offers admission one time a year.
Admissions are open from Playgroup to Middle classes.

Admission Policy:

If Three Siblings are enrolled from the same Family,
Only two will be charged the Monthly Fee, and one will be Free.

Admission Requirements:

  • The student must be accompanied by a guardian (Father, Elder Brother, Grand Father, Uncle etc..)
  • CNIC(Natinal ID Card) of the Parents/Guardian.
  • Active Contact Number must be provided.
  • If the Student transferring from another School, must provide a School Leaving Certificate(SLC)

Note:

We do not charge any Admission Fee.
